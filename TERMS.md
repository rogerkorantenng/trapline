## What the app does

TRAPLINE is a game that runs inside Reddit posts on the Devvit platform. It lets you race obstacle courses, build your own courses, and compete on leaderboards with other Reddit users.

## Your content

When you publish a course, propose a Gauntlet segment, or share a run result as a comment, you're creating content that other users can see and interact with. You're responsible for anything you submit.

You agree not to submit content that:
- violates Reddit's Content Policy or Devvit's Developer Rules
- is hateful, harassing, or directed at a specific person
- contains personal information about others without their consent

We can remove any course or content that violates these terms without notice.

## What we store

TRAPLINE stores the following in Devvit's Redis:
- courses you publish (tiles, title, your Reddit username)
- your leaderboard times and wipeout counts on each course
- ghost replay data for the current record on each course
- wipeout markers and taunts you leave during play
- your votes on Gauntlet proposals

We don't store passwords, email addresses, or any information beyond what Reddit already provides through your account.

## Ghost replays and leaderboards

Your run data (time, position frames, wipeout count) may be stored and shown to other players as a ghost or leaderboard entry. This is part of the core game. If you don't want your run stored, don't submit it.
