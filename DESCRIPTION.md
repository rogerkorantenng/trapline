TRAPLINE is a precision platformer where the community builds every level.

Race short, hard courses to the finish flag as fast as you can. Every course was made by another player using the built-in editor — you paint tiles, name your course, and publish it for the whole subreddit to race. Each course keeps a leaderboard and a ghost replay of whoever holds the record, so you're chasing a real person's exact line through the level, not a timer.

Wipe out and you leave a marker and a taunt at that exact spot. The next player sees everywhere people came unstuck before they even start. Finish a course and share your result as a real Reddit comment — your time and medal posted directly to the thread.

Every night a new daily course is picked automatically. The Gauntlet — one mega-course the whole subreddit builds together — gets a new section added each night based on player votes. There's always something new to race, and always a faster line to find.

Works on desktop and mobile.
